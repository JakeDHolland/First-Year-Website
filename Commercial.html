Weymouth FC Commercial Opportunities By Weymouth FC

Yearly Report 2014/2015

Weymouth FC Commercial Team are pleased to release the 2014-15 sponsorship and advertising opportunities. One opportunity is the 2014-15 Patrons' Draw which only £125 plus VAT to enter the draw.The top prize is sponsor/business name on the back of the home and away shirt which is valued at £1,750 plus VAT.
Also, everyone who enters will receive 1 free Business Match Ticket for 4 people to a home league game of their choice plus a 15% discount voucher to use against a hospitality package during the season. The draw will take place on Saturday 12th July in the Top Mark Carpets Lounge following the friendly v Guernsey,free entry for patrons' drawers.
The Commercial Team has tailored the sponsorship packages to appeal to small, medium and large businesses, as well as individuals, with prices starting from as little as £65 plus VAT. The hospitality packages could be used by businesses or maybe a present for a birthday surprise/special occasion, or just a group of friends/supporters who want to enjoy the whole match day experience.
We are also pleased to confirm that we are launching a new Executive Club this season with only ten places available. The Executive Club offers entry to all league home games, a match day programme, hospitality/entry into the Martyn Harrision Sponsors' Lounge, half-time refreshment and a car park pass. All for only £800 plus VAT (this can be paid monthly).
Weymouth FC has gone from strength to strength over the past few seasons in rebuilding the image and brand of the club commercially to both supporters and businesses alike. The commercial team has worked hard to ensure we deliver on our promises which means everyone receives the package they have chosen and paid for.
Weymouth FC is in good shape for the forthcoming season and Jason Matthews is assembling a superb squad. Jason keeps in regular contact with the Commercial Team.
The Board of Directors would like to thank every new or old business, supporter, and volunteer for all you have done, and are doing in putting Weymouth FC back where we belong
