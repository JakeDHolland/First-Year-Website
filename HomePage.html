Dorset Senior Cup Final, Tuesday 14 April 2015

Yesterday evening the County Cup Committee considered the request of both Finalists to toss a coin for choice of Venue.

The County Cup Committee agreed to the request, a coin was tossed during the Meeting and Weymouth won and will therefore host the Final.
